Join Us

The Earth Intelligence Lab (EIL) at The Chinese University of Hong Kong welcomes motivated students and researchers interested in Earth observation, artificial intelligence, carbon cycle science, climate risks, and environmental sustainability. For all positions, please contact Prof. Zherong Wu (zherongwu@cuhk.edu.hk) directly with your CV.

Ph.D

  • We are looking for self-motivated PhD students to join EIL in Spring/Fall 2027.
  • Applicants with backgrounds in Remote Sensing, Computer Science, Ecology, Environmental Science, Geography, or related fields are encouraged to apply.
  • Strong quantitative, programming, and scientific communication skills are highly valued.

Available funding schemes include:

  • Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS)
  • CUHK Vice-Chancellor's PhD Scholarship Scheme
  • Postgraduate Studentships (MPhil and PhD)

Postdoc

  • We are always looking for outstanding postdoctoral researchers interested in Earth observation, artificial intelligence, carbon cycle science, climate risks, and related areas. Applicants are expected to have an excellent publication record and demonstrated ability to conduct independent research.

Research Assistant

  • We welcome research assistants with backgrounds in Remote Sensing, Computer Science, Environmental Science, Geography, or related fields. Strong programming and data analysis skills are preferred.

Undergraduate Research Opportunities

  • We welcome curious and motivated undergraduate students to join the lab. Students will have opportunities to participate in ongoing research projects, explore research interests, and gain hands-on research experience.

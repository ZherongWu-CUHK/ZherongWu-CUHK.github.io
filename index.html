About Us

The Earth Intelligence Lab (EIL) integrates Earth observation, environmental science, and artificial intelligence to better understand our changing planet. Our research focuses on two grand challenges: understanding carbon dynamics across the Earth system and understanding climate risks in a changing world.

Carbon Intelligence

Understanding carbon dynamics and ecosystem responses in a changing climate.

Climate Intelligence

Understanding climate-driven hazards, extreme events, and their impacts on natural and human systems.

Earth Observation

Observing the Earth system through multi-source satellite remote sensing across space and time.

Artificial Intelligence

Developing AI methods to advance Earth system understanding and scientific discovery.

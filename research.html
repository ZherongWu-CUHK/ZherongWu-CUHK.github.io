Earth Intelligence Lab | CUHK

The Earth Intelligence Lab (EIL) integrates Earth observation, environmental science, and artificial intelligence to better understand the changing Earth. Our research focuses on two grand challenges: understanding the global carbon cycle and understanding climate risks in a changing world.

Featured Research

Carbon Intelligence

Current topics: terrestrial carbon cycle dynamics, vegetation dynamics, blue carbon, and anthropogenic carbon emissions.

Climate Intelligence

Current topics: land subsidence, landslides, floods, wildfires, and climate risk assessment.

Earth Observation

Current topics: SAR/InSAR remote sensing, optical remote sensing, and Earth system observations.

Artificial Intelligence

Current topics: knowledge-guided AI, scientific machine learning, and geospatial AI.
